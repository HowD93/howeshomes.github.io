About Us

Your Vision, Our Precision – at Howes Homes LLC, we bring experience and dedication to a wide variety of services, from remodeling and home updates to custom outdoor projects. Whether you’re looking to refresh a bathroom or kitchen, enhance your outdoor spaces with landscaping, custom-built gardening beds, or poured concrete pathways, or simply tackle seasonal tasks like Christmas light decorating, we’ve got you covered.

We also handle painting, roofing, siding, deck and fence construction, as well as building pergolas, stairs, and sheds. Need lock installation, rekeying, or assistance with a lockout? We’re here to help with those, too. Our expertise extends to practical outdoor projects like tree cutting, firewood chopping, trench digging, and ground leveling, along with hauling services for junk removal, dump runs, and material transportation.

With skills in custom furniture making and repair, pressure washing, and even creating custom fire pits, we’re ready to take on any project, big or small. Our goal is to provide reliable, high-quality solutions tailored to fit your unique needs.
